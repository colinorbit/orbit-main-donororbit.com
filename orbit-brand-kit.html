01 — Logo

Logo System

The Orbit logo consists of two elements: the orbital mark and the wordmark. They should always appear together. Never distort, recolor, or separate the elements.

Orbit.
Primary — Dark Background
Orbit.

Orbit.

Logo Rules
✓ Always
Use the full wordmark + mark together
Maintain clear space equal to the mark height
Use approved color variants only
Use SVG or high-res PNG for print
✕ Never
Stretch or distort the mark
Change logo colors outside approved variants
Add drop shadows or effects
Use below 24px height

07 — Voice & Tone

Voice & Tone

Orbit speaks like a trusted intelligence partner — confident, precise, and deeply respectful of the work advancement professionals do. Never hype. Never jargon.

Brand Personality
Confident
We make clear, direct statements. We don't hedge what Orbit does.
Precise
Specific language. Real capabilities. No invented metrics or vague promises.
Respectful
Gift officers are professionals. Orbit augments them — it never replaces them.
✓ Write Like This
✓"Orbit surfaces 10 predictive signals per donor — openly, not as a black box."
✓"Every AI action routes through your gift officers for approval."
✓"Built for private universities with major gift programs."
✓"Orbit augments your team. It never bypasses them."
✓"Four autonomous Virtual Officers. One advancement mission."
✕ Avoid This
✕"Revolutionary AI that transforms fundraising forever."
✕"10x your gift officer productivity instantly."
✕"Powered by cutting-edge machine learning algorithms."
✕"Replaces your advancement team with smart automation."
✕"The future of fundraising is here."
