The Platform

Four Virtual Officers.
One advancement mission.

How It Works

Intelligence that moves
through your office.

From data to approved action — Orbit handles the work between the signals and the gift officer.

01
🔗
Connect Your Data
Orbit ingests structured data from your existing CRM — Blackbaud, Ellucian, or Salesforce — along with gift history, engagement records, and wealth screening feeds.
02
◎
Score Every Constituent
The Predictive Intelligence Layer scores each donor across 10 behavioral signals, surfacing who needs attention, when to ask, how much, and through which channel.
03
⚡
Officers Draft Actions
VEO, VSO, VPGO, and VCO each monitor their domain and draft outreach, reports, and recommendations — routed into the approval queue for your team.
04
✓
Your Team Approves
Gift officers review, refine, and approve every action. Nothing reaches a donor without human authorization. Orbit executes; your team leads.
